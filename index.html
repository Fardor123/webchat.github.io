How to use:

  1. Click "Generate New Keys" to create a new key pair
  2. Share the Public Key with your group
  3. Keep your Private Key secret (it won't be sent to the server)
  4. Enter your name and click "Join Chat"

Note: All messages are encrypted and only stored in your browser.
